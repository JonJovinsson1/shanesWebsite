Our Services

We provide a full range of concrete services including durable driveways, low-maintenance liquid limestone for pool surrounds and patios, high-gloss polished concrete, and elegant honed finishes. We also offer exposed aggregate for added texture and traction, coloured concrete in a variety of tones, and slip-resistant pool decks. Our team handles resurfacing and overlays to renew existing slabs, as well as structural slabs and foundations, cast-in-place or precast stairs and steps, and reinforced concrete for maximum strength.
